About Me

I was born and raised in Massachusetts and grew up in a small town about 45 minutes south of Boston. I have two brothers, one of which is a Software Engineer as well! Growing up, I played soccer at a high level and enjoyed spending time with my friends and family. I love being active and going to the gym too!

I am currently a full time student at Coding Temple and very excited to gain knowledge and pursue Software Development as a career. I decided to pursue this career after having worked as a General Manager in gyms for most of my 20's. What excites me the most is the amount of possibilities and innovative things you can do with software development. I look forward to this exciting journey!
